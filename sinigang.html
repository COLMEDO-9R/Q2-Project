Sinigang na Baboy

A comforting sour soup made with pork, vegetables, and tamarind. Perfect for rainy days!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork (belly or ribs, cut into chunks)
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 3 long green chili peppers
  • 1 packet of sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind if available)
  • 2 cups water spinach (kangkong)
  • 1 cup eggplant, sliced
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1/2 cup string beans
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Follow these steps to make a perfect Sinigang na Baboy:

  1. In a pot, combine pork, onion, tomatoes, and enough water to cover the ingredients. Boil for about 45 minutes or until the pork is tender.
  2. Add the sinigang mix (or tamarind), green chili peppers, fish sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir and let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add the vegetables (kangkong, eggplant, radish, and string beans) and cook until tender, about 5-10 minutes.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning with fish sauce or salt if necessary. Serve hot with rice!
