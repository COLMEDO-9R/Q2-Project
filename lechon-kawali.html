Lechon Kawali

Crispy, deep-fried pork belly, served with a delicious dipping sauce. A favorite Filipino dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into large chunks
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 5 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorns
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Vinegar and soy sauce for dipping sauce

Instructions

Follow these steps to make the crispy and delicious Lechon Kawali:

  1. In a large pot, combine the pork belly, onion, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, and salt. Add enough water to cover the pork and bring it to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for about 1-1.5 hours, or until the pork is tender. Let the pork cool.
  3. Once cooled, pat the pork belly dry with paper towels.
  4. In a deep pot, heat enough oil for frying. Fry the pork belly in batches until crispy and golden brown, about 8-10 minutes per batch.
  5. Serve with a dipping sauce made from vinegar and soy sauce. Enjoy with steamed rice!
