Adobo Recipe

A savory and flavorful Filipino dish made with chicken or pork in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken or pork (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Follow these simple steps to make delicious Filipino Adobo:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken or pork, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. In a large skillet or pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the marinated meat and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the marinade (soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, and spices) to the pan. Stir well and bring to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, lower the heat and simmer for 40 minutes, or until the meat is tender and the sauce has thickened. Add water if the sauce becomes too thick.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
